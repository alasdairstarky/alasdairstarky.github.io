Tarhuna, is a well equipped 3-bedroom holiday cottage, located in Aultgrishan, Melvaig, 9 miles north of Gairloch, North-West Highlands of Scotland. The cottage is set back from the main road, in a large garden overlooking the Minch. You can enjoy fantastic views of The Isle of Skye, Harris and Lewis, with the Shiant Isles in the foreground and stunning sunsets. Sheep graze on the surrounding croft land where there is an abundance of flaura and fauna. Please visit our booking page if you would like to make a reservation online.
