Accommodation



  • Entrance porch with ample storage and hanging space
  • Utility room with washing machine, dryer.
  • Large bright comfortable lounge and dining room with TV, DVD and Radio/CD player, with patio doors leading into the garden.
  • Fully equipped, breakfasting kitchen with fridge freezer, dishwasher, microwave, toaster, sandwich toaster, and a good range of dishes and cooking pots and utensils.
  • 1 double bedroom with en-suite toilet and shower.
  • 2 twin bedded rooms.
  • Bathroom with electric shower over bath.
  • All bedding and towels are included in the price.

All accommodation is on the ground floor. There is ramp access and all rooms are accessible to wheelchair users.
